Invertis Innovation & Incubation

This is the home for the thriving community of Invertis' Innovators & Entrepreneurs.

Introducing

III is not merely a building; it is the vibrant core of the innovation and entrepreneurship (I&E) community at Invertis. This space represents the result of years of meticulous planning, extensive reconstruction, and national community-building. Situated in the heart of Invertis University Bareilly, known as "the most innovative place in the state," III fosters opportunities for transformative impact, not just at Invertis, but across the Indian innovation ecosystem and beyond.

By Hon'ble Chancellor Sir

At Invertis Innovation and Incubation, we are nurturing innovation to empower the next generation of entrepreneurs. Our mission is to provide essential resources, mentorship, and a vibrant ecosystem that transforms ideas into impactful businesses on both local and global scales. Our commitment is to support aspiring entrepreneurs every step of the way, ensuring they have the tools and guidance needed to succeed in today's competitive landscape. Join us on this journey to unlock the potential of innovation and bring their visionary ideas to life.

Dr. Umesh Gautam

The thrust area of incubation at our center focuses on nurturing innovative startups in key domains such as technology, healthcare, sustainability, and social impact. We provide comprehensive support through mentorship, funding, and resources to help early-stage ventures grow and succeed in a dynamic market.
